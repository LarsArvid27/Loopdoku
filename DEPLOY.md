Visit: **https://LarsArvid27.github.io/loopdoku/**

## How to Enable GitHub Pages (if not auto-enabled)

1. Go to your repository: https://github.com/LarsArvid27/loopdoku
2. Click **Settings** (top menu)
3. Scroll down to **Pages** section
4. Under "Source", select **Deploy from a branch**
5. Choose **main** branch
6. Click **Save**

Your game will be live in a few seconds! 🎵
